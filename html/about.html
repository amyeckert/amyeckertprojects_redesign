I am an artist and photographer based outside of Philadelphia, PA. My commercial work and the personal projects I pursue reflect a keen interest in people and how they live. I am deeply attentive to the details that make and project more nuanced, more engaging and more effective. Intelligence of vision, depth of experience, and a big sense of humor are what I bring to every assignment. Most of all, I love working with a creative and motivated team!

My work has been published widely by a diverse range of editorial and commercial clients, including:

The New York Times Magazine, Southern Poverty Law Center, Minnesota Farmers Union, Martha Stewart Living, The Blue Man Group, House and Garden, Scientific American, American Lawyer, Life, GHI, Time, HSBC, Dwell, InStyle, Walmart, Microsoft, Fast Company

See my photography assignment work at www.amyeckertphoto.com.
