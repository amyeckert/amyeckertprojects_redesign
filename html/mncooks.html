For two years in a row I had the great pleasure of photographing organic, sustainable farmers and chefs all over the great state of Minnesota. I fell in love with the state and its people (and food) making these pictures for the MN Farmer's Union and Food Alliance Midwest.

The Minnesota Cooks Calendar features the year's participating chefs, the farmers who supply them, and a delicious recipe every month. For more information, visist www.minnestoacooks.net and support local farmers wherever you live.
